Best Place to stay in Chikmagalur (Mudigere)


Astagiri Homestay is for the people who love nature and want to be a part of it. Stay at this elegant home in Western Ghats at Chikmaglur to enjoy traditional culture, food, dance, agriculture and exchange the unique culture of this huge district. We have opened the doors of our home stay to hundreds of guests who live luxuriously in the Houses, rooms, cottages amidst lush green coffee estates and make the memorable moments of their life. Tourists get fascinated by colorful scenery, wooded slopes, undulating landscape and quaint villages of Chikmagalur and Sakleshpur. There are endless mountain ranges which attract thousands of tourists every year to our homestay.
